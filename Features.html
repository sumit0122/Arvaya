Clinic Management

The Arvaya app streamlines doctor-patient interactions with an efficient Appointment Scheduling system, allowing doctors to manage available slots and update their availability seamlessly. It also enables comprehensive management of patient consultations, billing, and secure data access.

  • Appointment Scheduling : Provide functionality for doctors to manage appointment slots, including slot generation and availability updates.
  • Consultation Record Management : Enable doctors to create, view, and update patient consultation records, including prescriptions and follow-up notes.
  • Patient Data Access : Allow doctors to securely access and view comprehensive patient profiles and medical histories during consultations.
  • Billing Management : Implement billing functionality that allows doctors to generate invoices for consultations and track payments.

Pathology Services

The Arvaya app offers robust Test Management features, enabling lab assistants to manage both independent and packaged tests, schedule sample collection, and generate reports. The app also allows for seamless tracking of patient test requests, results, and follow-up actions, as well as secure billing and invoicing.

  • Test Management : Enable lab assistants to manage both independent and packaged tests, including scheduling, sample collection, and report generation.
  • Patient Management : Provide functionality to track patient test requests, results, and any follow-up actions required.
  • Reporting System : Develop a system for generating and securely sharing lab reports with patients and healthcare providers.
  • Billing and Invoicing : Implement a billing system for lab tests, allowing for easy invoice generation and payment tracking.

Pharmacy Management

The Arvaya app provides comprehensive Drug Inventory Management, empowering pharmacists to track stock levels, monitor expiry dates, and manage orders. The app also allows for secure processing of prescriptions and access to patient medication histories to ensure safe dispensing.

  • Drug Inventory Management : Track stock levels, reordering, and expiry dates.
  • Order Management : Process medication orders and manage incoming supply orders from suppliers.
  • Patient Management : Access patient profiles, medication history, and consultation records.
  • Prescription Processing : Receive, validate, and dispense prescriptions with safety alerts for drug interactions and allergies.
