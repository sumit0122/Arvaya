Drug Inventory Management

The Arvaya app offers comprehensive Drug Inventory Management, empowering pharmacists to track stock levels, reorder medications, and monitor expiry dates to ensure timely availability. The Order Management feature streamlines patient medication orders and manages incoming supply orders from suppliers, enhancing operational efficiency. Pharmacists can access patient profiles, medication histories, and consultation records through the Patient Management system, ensuring safe and informed dispensing. Prescription Processing capabilities include receiving, validating, and dispensing prescriptions with built-in alerts for potential drug interactions or allergies, promoting patient safety. These features make Arvaya an essential tool for modern pharmacy management.
