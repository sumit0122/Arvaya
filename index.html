ABDM Vision

The ABDM aims to establish a comprehensive digital healthcare ecosystem, ensuring seamless access and management of personal health records and healthcare services.

  • Digital access to healthcare services across the country.
  • Efficient and secure health data exchange.
  • Empowerment of individuals with their health data.

Benefits of ABDM

ABDM brings transformative changes to healthcare delivery by integrating digital tools, making healthcare accessible and efficient for all.

  • Unified health record across different platforms.
  • Improved healthcare quality through data-driven insights.
  • Reduced redundancy and healthcare costs.

What is UHI?

The Unified Health Interface (UHI) is a key component of ABDM, enabling interoperable health services across various healthcare providers.

  • A standardized interface for healthcare services.
  • Allows seamless health data transfer between providers.
  • Promotes accessibility and choice for patients.

Benefits for UHI

UHI provides numerous advantages for both patients and healthcare providers by streamlining digital health interactions.

  • Enhances patient choice and access to care.
  • Enables efficient coordination of healthcare services.
  • Supports health data security and privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, features, and how to get started with the Arvaya app. We are here to help you with your health management journey.

What is a PHR App?

A Personal Health Record (PHR) App allows users to access and manage their personal health records digitally. It enables easy linking and sharing of health information with healthcare providers, improving continuity of care.

What is an ABHA number?

ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) number is a unique 14-digit identifier assigned to individuals as part of India's digital health mission. It allows users to link, access, and share their health records securely across the healthcare ecosystem.

What is an ABHA address?

An ABHA address is a unique username assigned to an individual that allows seamless sharing and management of health records. It works similarly to an email address and is used alongside the ABHA number to facilitate secure data exchange.

What is HIP?

HIP (Health Information Provider) refers to any healthcare facility, hospital, clinic, or lab that holds health records and can provide them in a digital format. HIPs are responsible for sharing a patient's health information with authorized recipients as per their consent.

What is HIU?

HIU (Health Information User) is an entity or organization, like a doctor, clinic, or hospital, that needs access to a patient’s health records to provide medical care. HIUs can request health information from HIPs with the user's consent.

How do I create an ABHA number?

You can create an ABHA number through the PHR app by registering with your mobile number and verifying your identity. This process involves authentication through an Aadhaar or PAN card to ensure the security of your personal data.

How do I link my health records with my ABHA number?

Once you have an ABHA number, you can use the PHR app to link health records from various providers. You can request data from providers, view linked records, and share them with healthcare professionals.

How can I manage my data-sharing consent?

The PHR app allows you to manage your data-sharing consent directly. You can approve or deny requests from healthcare providers to access your records and set permissions on what information they can view or update.

How can doctors access my health records?

Doctors or healthcare providers can access your health records through the PHR app with your consent. You’ll receive a notification to approve or deny each data access request, ensuring that you remain in control of your personal information.

What are the benefits of using the PHR app?

The PHR app provides easy access to your health records in one place, which can enhance the quality of care by allowing healthcare professionals to review your history quickly. It also empowers you to take charge of your health data and manage consent for sharing information.
